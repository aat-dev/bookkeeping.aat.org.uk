Introducing bookkeeping membership

Our bookkeeping membership offers you the opportunity to gain professional recognition for the hard work you've put into your studies. As an AAT Bookkeeper you’ll gain the designatory letters AATQB, demonstrating your skills to employers, colleagues and clients.

Why you should become an AATQB

  • Give your career a boost

    The designatory letters AATQB demonstrate your educational achievements and your commitment to keeping your professional skills up to date. It could help you secure your perfect job or gain a promotion.

  • Run your own business

    AATQB gives you the opportunity to become self-employed, offering bookkeeping services in the UK with a recognised brand behind you. Regulated and supported by AAT, we can help you to fulfil your dreams.

  • Keep your skills sharp

    As an AAT Bookkeeper you'll gain access to all the information you need to keep on top of the latest updates in finance. Our newsletters will provide you with topical information, while our online resources and events will help you keep your skills fresh.

  • Join our community

    Our professional membership includes over 50,000 AATQBs, MAATs and FMAATs. Share experiences, learn from others and meet likeminded people online and at events.

Who can apply?

  • An AAT student who has completed the Advanced Certificate in Bookkeeping or the Advanced Diploma in Accounting – Level 3. *

  • Affiliates who completed the AAT Accounting Qualification on the 2010, 2013 or 2016 standards.

  • Current full (MAAT) and fellow (FMAAT) members.

  • A member of an approved professional body, including IAB and ICB.
    Find out more.

*

Students studying the Level 3 Certificate in Bookkeeping and Ethics before September 2016 will need to complete the additional Indirect Tax unit to be eligible. We’ve made this really easy and cost-effective for you. Please contact us to discuss your options.

If you are a student continuing your studies, your student membership is included within this cost. If you are a full or fellow member, bookkeeping membership is included within your current membership at no additional cost.
